Fried Egg

Fried eggs are most favourtie meals > I always love to eat it

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • oil
  • salt
  • papper

Method:

  1. break the eggs
  2. heat the oil on medium heat on the pan
  3. sprinkle some salt on it
  4. then some papper
  5. wait till it's done
  6. eat it

Taste :

It tastes so good. sugoyyyyyyyyy!!!!!
